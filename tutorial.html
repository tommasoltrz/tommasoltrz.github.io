TUTORIALS

In a nutshell

At Arduino I was content creator for several projects, kits and products. As a company founded on the Open Source phylosophy creating tutorials for the community is a big part of a product's launch. I beta tested and created content for Arduino Cloud, Create, project hub, the mkr1000 board and the IoT bundle.

Here I gathered a list of some of the tutorials I shared with the community so far. Most of them can be found on the Arduino Project Hub.

All the projects are easily replicable with basic FabLab equipment, such as laser-cutter machines and 3D-printers.
